## Contributing — We Welcome Your Input!

The Juvenile Fin-Clamp is designed as an evolving, community-driven open 
hardware tool. We actively encourage researchers who use, test, or modify 
the design to share their experiences and suggestions.

### How to contribute:
- **Report a problem** — If you encounter issues during production or deployment, 
  open a [GitHub Issue](../issues) describing the problem
- **Suggest an improvement** — Have an idea for a design improvement or 
  adaptation for a different species or fin type? Open an Issue and describe 
  your suggestion
- **Share your results** — Used the fin-clamp in your research? We would love 
  to hear about it — open an Issue or get in touch directly

Your contributions help improve this tool for the entire research community.
